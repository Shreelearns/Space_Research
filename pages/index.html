About SpaceLink Research

Discover our mission, vision, and the passionate team driving humanity's future in space.

Our Origin Story

Founded in 2008 by a group of visionary scientists and engineers, SpaceLink Research emerged from a shared belief that humanity's future lies among the stars. What began as a small research initiative has grown into a globally recognized space research organization.

Our founders asked a simple but profound question: "Why limit our exploration to Earth when the universe offers infinite possibilities?" This question became the driving force behind our organization's creation and continues to inspire our work today.

From our humble beginnings in a converted warehouse with just 12 employees, we've expanded to multiple research facilities across three continents, employing over 2,000 dedicated professionals who share our passion for space exploration.

Our Vision in Action

Mission & Vision

Our Mission: To advance human knowledge and capability in space through innovative research, cutting-edge technology, and international collaboration, making space accessible and beneficial for all humanity.

Our Vision: A future where humanity thrives as a multi-planetary species, where space exploration drives technological advancement and inspires new generations to reach for the stars.

We believe that by pushing the boundaries of what's possible in space, we can address critical challenges on Earth, from climate monitoring to resource management, while expanding human presence throughout the solar system.
